Konark Sun Temple

The Konark Sun Temple is a 13th-century Hindu temple dedicated to the sun god Surya. It is located in Konark, Odisha, India. The temple is designed in the shape of a colossal chariot with 24 wheels, each about 10 feet in diameter, and is drawn by seven horses.

Jagannath Puri Temple

The Jagannath Temple in Puri is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Jagannath, a form of Vishnu. It is located in Puri, Odisha, India. The temple is an important pilgrimage destination and is particularly known for its annual Rath Yatra, or chariot festival.

Jagannath Puri Temple

Lingaraj Temple

The Lingaraj Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Harihara, a form of Shiva and Vishnu. It is one of the oldest and largest temples in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, and is a major pilgrimage destination for devotees of Lord Shiva.

Lingaraj Temple

Kamakhya Temple

The Kamakhya Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Kamakhya, a form of Shakti. It is one of the oldest and most sacred temples in Guwahati, Assam, and is an important pilgrimage destination, especially during the Ambubachi Mela.

Kamakhya Temple

Shirdi Sai Baba Temple

The Shirdi Sai Baba Temple is a shrine dedicated to Sai Baba, a revered spiritual leader who lived in the town of Shirdi in Maharashtra, India. The temple attracts millions of devotees from all over the world who come to seek blessings and solace.

Shirdi Sai Baba Temple

Kalighat Kali Temple

The Kalighat Kali Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Kali. It is one of the 51 Shakti Peethas and is located in Kolkata, West Bengal, India. The temple is a revered pilgrimage site and is visited by devotees throughout the year.

Kalighat Kali Temple

Vishnupad Temple

The Vishnupad Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu. It is located in Gaya, Bihar, India, and is believed to be the spot where Lord Vishnu's footprints are imprinted in solid rock. The temple is an important pilgrimage site for Hindus.
