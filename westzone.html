Siddhivinayak Temple

The Siddhivinayak Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesh. It is located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, and is one of the richest temples in Mumbai.

Dwarakadhish Temple

The Dwarakadhish Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna. It is located in Dwarka, Gujarat, India, and is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites.

Somnath Temple

The Somnath Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is located in Prabhas Patan near Veraval in Saurashtra, Gujarat, India.

Ranakpur Temple

The Ranakpur Temple is a Jain temple dedicated to Tirthankara Adinatha. It is located in Ranakpur, Rajasthan, India, and is known for its intricate marble carvings and architectural beauty.

Dilwara Temples

The Dilwara Temples are a group of Jain temples known for their extraordinary architecture and intricate marble carvings. They are located in Mount Abu, Rajasthan, India.

Bhuleshwar Temple

The Bhuleshwar Temple is an ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is located near Pune, Maharashtra, India, and is known for its unique architecture and historical significance.
