Vaishno Devi Temple

Located in Katra, Jammu and Kashmir, Vaishno Devi Temple is dedicated to the Hindu goddess Mata Vaishno Devi. It is one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in India.

Vaishno Devi Temple

Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib)

Situated in Amritsar, Punjab, the Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, is a significant religious and cultural symbol for Sikhs. It attracts millions of visitors from all over the world.

Golden Temple

Kedarnath Temple

Located in the Himalayan range of Uttarakhand, Kedarnath Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites and holds great religious significance.

Kedarnath Temple

Badrinath Temple

Situated in the Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand, Badrinath Temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu. It is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites and is considered one of the holiest Hindu temples.

Badrinath Temple

Hemkund Sahib

Located in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand, Hemkund Sahib is a Sikh shrine associated with Guru Gobind Singh Ji. It is situated at an altitude of 4,632 meters and is accessible by trekking.

Hemkund Sahib

Rishikesh Temples

Rishikesh, located in Uttarakhand, is known as the 'Yoga Capital of the World' and is home to various temples including Neelkanth Mahadev Temple and Triveni Ghat, which attract spiritual seekers and tourists alike.

Rishikesh Temples

Mathura and Vrindavan Temples

Mathura and Vrindavan, located in Uttar Pradesh, are associated with the life of Lord Krishna. Temples like Banke Bihari Temple and ISKCON Temple attract devotees from all over the world.

Mathura and Vrindavan Temples

Varanasi Temples

Varanasi, also known as Kashi, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and is considered the spiritual capital of India. Temples like Kashi Vishwanath Temple and Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple are major attractions.

Varanasi Temples

Ayodhya Temples

Ayodhya, situated in Uttar Pradesh, is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama. Temples like Ram Janmabhoomi Temple and Hanuman Garhi Temple are significant pilgrimage sites for Hindus.

Ayodhya Temples
